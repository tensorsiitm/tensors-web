WHAT IS DE-COV IDEATHON ?



      Tensors in association with the Indian Medical Association, Kerala conducted the DeCov Ideathon. COVID-19 has struck every corner of this world and the entire human race is under strings of this deadly pandemic. Ideas and Innovation has empowered us ever since. The human brain can achieve impossible feats. Ideathon was conducted with an aim to find solutions for preventing the spread of the current pandemic and for recovering the global economy in the post-Corona period. The cash prize was 50K, also the best solutions and ideas will be sent to the government for the best possible implementation.

      The Pandemic has struck at the resources and skills of global population and made all incumbent systems redundant. Helplessness and fear is spread wide and requires active minds to work on solutions to cope with issues in a Corona and Post-Corona phase. Being a holistic issue affecting all possible human domains, this requires multi lateral thinking and solutions inclusive of all sects. The De-Cov challenge aims to tap into the most intelligent minds and citizens of the country to find solutions and provide channels for them to officially take it up to the concerned authorities. The Challenge was focussed on the following four channels:
1.Health Care   2.Community   3.Economy and Business   4.Accessiibility
